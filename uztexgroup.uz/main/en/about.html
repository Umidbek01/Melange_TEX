The textile industry of Uzbekistan is constantly developing, offering consumers high-quality modern products and is strategically important for the economy of Uzbekistan. Now the demand for textiles in the world market, its importance and role for the economies of countries focused on its production is already being compared with the positions of oil. Until recently, our country exported raw cotton. Gradually, introducing new technologies and processes, they switched to the production of cotton fiber and every year the share of its exports is decreasing, and by 2021 Uzbekistan plans to completely stop its exports in favor of complete processing and production of finished products.

Textiles, as well as such basic raw materials as oil and gas, are widely used in the world production of consumer goods, without which our life is simply unthinkable. The prosperity of this industry is also facilitated by the presence of Uzbekistan's rich own resources, which provide environmentally friendly, 100% natural raw materials, as well as created over the years of development of light industry, in particular textile, a large number of both state and private enterprises, the equipment and production capacity of which meeting all world standards, they allow to raise Uzbek textiles to the level of a strategic resource capable of influencing world markets.

Today, more than 7000 light industry enterprises operate in Uzbekistan.

Among them, the company "MELANGE UZTEX GROUP" is a leading, vertically integrated manufacturer of textile products in the CIS countries. The production facilities of "MELANGE UZTEX GROUP" allow to manufacture the whole range of textile products from cotton: all types of harsh and dyed yarns, harsh and dyed fabrics, ready-made knitwear, hosiery, terry products.

Our company, having launched the first factory for the production of painted canvas "MELANGE UZTEX CHIRCHIK" 11 years ago, due to the fact that we feel strong government support for entrepreneurial initiatives, in particular, tax and customs preferences, lending on favorable terms and others, has expanded to a powerful production group consisting of 13 factories located throughout Uzbekistan. Therefore, our enterprises are gaining strength, releasing huge potential in the field of processing and production of finished products, which inspires confidence that with the task set in accordance with the decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to further develop light industry and stimulate the production of finished products" and the transition from export raw cotton for the export of already processed cotton fiber and finished products, we can handle it.
